In my statics course, I was tasked with designing a first project involving force equilibrium, reaction forces, and moment calculations. This homework served as the foundation for this larger portfolio assignment in which we are developing a digital statics portfolio for engineers. The goal is to translate traditional hand-calculated solutions into an organized, interactive platform that showcases both the analytical process and final results.
